Hack the city 1.0

City University's first-ever hackathon, organized by the Computer Science Society, will take place on January 27th, 2024. We aim to bring together open-minded and innovative programmers from around the world, providing them with an opportunity to step away from the typical coursework environment and tackle global issues through creative and innovative programming ideas.

Our tickets will be on sale on first of December. Make sure to grab them early because they're expected to sell out very fast. If you don't want to miss out you can sign up to the pre-sale and recieve an email as soon as the tickets open.

How much do the tickets cost?

Nothing! They're absolutely free.

Do I need to have a team?

You don't need to have a team but it's highly recommended. If you don't have a team you can still come and we can help you find one!

Who can attend?

Anyone who goes to university, or has graduated a year ago, or is going to go to university next year.

What programming languages can I use?

Believe it or not you don't need to know any programming! The hackathon is about creativity and innovation. You can use anything you know and enjoy working with and create whatever you want!
